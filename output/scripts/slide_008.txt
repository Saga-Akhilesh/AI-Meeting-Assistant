Good morning. Today, we're discussing 'What is a Stock Market?' Essentially, it’s an electronic platform for buying and selling shares of public companies. In India, our major exchanges are the Bombay Stock Exchange (1875, Sensex) and the National Stock Exchange (1992, Nifty 50). Its purpose is to channel savings into businesses, generate wealth, and historically, beat inflation over time, with access strictly through SEBI-registered brokers.
